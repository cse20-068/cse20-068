Arts & Culture

You’ve heard of London and Edinburgh, but it’s not just Britain’s best known cities that carry the culture torch. Thanks to a dazzlingly rich history, the UK is packed with more literature, arts and culture than you could absorb in a lifetime. Just take a look at our museums, theatres, and other cultural attractions in towns and cities all over the country. You’re bound to find a bit of culture in every corner!

Eating Out

You’ll be spoilt for choice with places to eat in the UK, with a wealth of great eating establishments ranging from Michelin starred restaurants to trendy street markets. With the impact of social media, the food industry in the UK has seen a high demand for new trendy and health conscious eateries across the country. With so many options, you’ll be guaranteed to find somewhere to match your budget and lifestyle. Head to Gordan Ramsey for an upscale dinner, Betty’s Tea Rooms for the finest selection of cakes or Farm Girl café for a gluten-free brunch.
